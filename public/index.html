About My Work at Camilyo

Camilio was a company whose flagship product was called SmartSite. SmartSite generated marketing websites for businesses. These sites could be created with a few clicks of a button. SmartSite also enabled choosing design, editing content, and adding digital storefront easily. My main job was to build various templates that the site contained and to update the editing system. The designs were prepared by Valeria Abramov. I used CSS, HTML, JSON and internal tools. I worked together with product and R&D departments. Unfortunately, Camilio closed down and now it is impossible to create websites on SmartSite or see my works directly.
